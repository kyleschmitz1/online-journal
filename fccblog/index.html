A Finch

An Object Lesson

MAR. 24, 2021

Finches are cute. Lex Fridman I may need to begin a more personal journal, but for now this one where I share somewhat helpful and insightful thoughts will have to do.

OK, I'LL BEGIN THE STORY NOW

One day, I saw a small yellow finch in a tree. He (It was bright yellow so I'm assuming it was a male) was about 30 yards away, perched about 20 feet above the ground. I figure this story could be a good object lesson to use to convince people to not drink alcohol. Like I aimed and shot at the bird not expecting to kill the bird, we tend to drink with every intention to not become an alcoholic, and where does that get us?
